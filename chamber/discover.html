Activites

Upcoming Activities:

  • HEMA Sword Training

    Historical European Martial Arts (HEMA) sword training sessions where participants learn the art of swordsmanship. Practitioners hone their skills with longswords, rapiers, and other medieval weapons, mastering techniques used by knights of old.

  • Archery Contest

    Marksmen and women showcase their precision and dexterity with the bow and arrow. Competitors aim for the bullseye, demonstrating their hunting and warfare skills in a thrilling competition of accuracy and focus.

  • Medieval Dance Revelry

    Festive gatherings where nobles and commoners alike enjoy lively music and traditional dances. These social events are filled with vibrant costumes, intricate footwork, and the joyous spirit of the medieval community.

Events

Upcoming Events:

  • Chess Tournament

    A strategic battle of wits where noblemen and scholars compete in the ancient game of chess. Participants employ tactics and foresight to outmaneuver their opponents, showcasing their intellectual prowess and mastery of the game.

  • Grand Jousting Tournament

    The ultimate test of knightly skill and bravery, where armored knights charge at each other with lances in a dramatic display of courage and combat prowess. Spectators gather to cheer on their champions in a spectacle of chivalry.

  • Blacksmith Competition

    Skilled blacksmiths demonstrate their craftsmanship in forging weapons, armor, and tools. Competitors are judged on their technique, creativity, and the quality of their creations, celebrating the essential artisans of the medieval world.
