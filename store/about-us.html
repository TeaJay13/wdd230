About Us

Welcome to MountainQuest Gear, your number one source for all things outdoor. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of outdoor gear, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2024 by TJ Strickland, MountainQuest Gear has come a long way from its beginnings in Salt Lake City, UT. When TJ first started out, his passion for eco-friendly outdoor products drove him to do tons of research, so that MountainQuest Gear can offer you the world's most advanced outdoor gear. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, sustainable, and reliable outdoor gear to adventurers worldwide. We strive to inspire a love for the outdoors and a commitment to preserving our natural environment.

At MountainQuest Gear, we believe in the power of nature to restore and rejuvenate the human spirit. Our products are designed to help you explore the great outdoors with confidence and respect for the environment.
